# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple rule: be direct, respectful, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Discuss technical disagreements with evidence.
- Assume good faith unless there is clear reason not to.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Respect privacy and do not post private data, user uploads, or secrets.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private keys, private user data, or copyrighted text without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Abusive language toward contributors or users.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may lead to blocked participation.
